The EDM World

Electronic Dance Music, commonly referred to as EDM, has grown tremendously over the past two decades. What started as a niche electronic scene has evolved into a global movement that brings together millions of fans across festivals, clubs, and online communities. I got into EDM at a very young age when my sister showed me Calvin Harris in 4th grade. Calvin Harris, along with others like David Guetta, took the genre to the masses helping expose many to a genre they had little understanding of before. One of the reasons I enjoy EDM so much is the free-spirited, non-judgemental environment and crowd the genre has traditionally attracted. While EDM is an umbrella term for a swath of other electronic-based genres such as house, drum n bass, techno, trance, dubstep, and more, the larger genre is at an all-time high in popularity. The next question will be if and how it can sustain itself while the adaption has brought negative factors that come with popularity: superficiaility, statusism, elistism, and loss of underground nature. However many artists are still being unique energy and creativity to the space. The hope is EDM escapes the mistakes of the disco era, which faced a counterculture and adoption from corporation ruining the artistic essence. For me, EDM represents more than just music. It represents a culture centered around creativity, community, and expression. Festivals and concerts often bring together people from many different backgrounds who share a common love for music. The atmosphere is incredibly positive and welcoming, which is part of what makes the experience so memorable. I also appreciate the artistic side of EDM production. Many artists spend years perfecting their sound design, mixing, and production techniques. The result is music that is both technically impressive and emotionally powerful. Whether it is progressive house, techno, or melodic bass, each subgenre brings a different mood and experience to listeners. A few current artists worthy of watching is Mall Grab and MPH.
